Squat

A squat is a fundamental lower body exercise that targets the muscles of the thighs, hips, and buttocks, specifically the quadriceps, hamstrings, and gluteal muscles. It also engages the core and lower back muscles.

Push-up

A push-up is a bodyweight exercise that primarily targets the muscles of the chest, shoulders, triceps, and core.

It is performed in a prone position, lying face down, and involves raising and lowering the body using the arms.

Crunch

A crunch is a popular abdominal exercise that primarily targets the rectus abdominis muscle, commonly known as the "six-pack" muscles. It's a staple exercise in many fitness routines due to its simplicity and effectiveness in strengthening the core.
